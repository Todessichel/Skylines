Main notes

  • Connect the Highway entry and exit

  • Power the water pumps with windmills
  • Build powerplants early to ensure energy availability
  • Build a cementery ASAP
  • Build a trash disposal ASAP
  • Build a police stations ASAP
  • Build a fire station ASAP
  • Increase the taxes for all areas to 13%
